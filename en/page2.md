# Beef preparation

1. Peel the carrots and slice them into rounds.  
2. Peel the onions.  
3. Peel, remove the germ, and chop the garlic clove.  
4. Cut the bacon into lardons.  
5. Cut the meat into pieces.  
6. Pour the oil into a large casserole dish and add half of the butter. Heat over medium heat and brown the onions and lardons without burning them.  
7. Remove the onions and lardons from the casserole dish, then put the meat pieces in their place. Brown them on all sides, sprinkle with flour, stir, then add the onions and lardons back in.  
8. Season with salt and pepper and pour in the red wine. Add the sliced carrots, chopped garlic, and bouquet garni, then cover and reduce the heat. Simmer for about 2 hours.  
9. Clean the mushrooms without soaking them in water, then dry them. Melt the remaining butter in a pan and sauté the mushrooms. Season with salt and pepper, add the tomato paste, and stir.  
10. About ten minutes before the end of cooking, add the mushrooms and their sauce to the casserole dish. Stir gently, remove the bouquet garni, and serve hot.

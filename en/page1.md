# French fries preparation

[Back to home](./) · [Go to page 2](page2.html)

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3ff148d0-c1c6-4cae-8767-3ffff468ec84)

**Step 1:** Peel the potatoes.  
**Step 2:** Soak them in cold water for a few minutes.  
**Step 3:** Rinse them thoroughly.  
**Step 4:** Cut the potatoes into sticks about 1 cm thick, keeping them fairly long.  
**Step 5:** Rinse the fries again under running water to remove the starch.  
**Step 6:** Dry them carefully with a clean kitchen towel.

### Follow the steps below

**Step 1:** Sauté the chopped garlic cloves and the sliced onions in a little olive oil.  
**Step 2:** Add the carrot and the chopped celery stalk, then the meat, and brown everything.  
**Step 3:** After a few minutes, add the red wine. Cook until it evaporates.  
**Step 4:** Add the tomato purée, water, and herbs. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer on low heat for 45 minutes.  
**Step 5:** Prepare the béchamel sauce: melt 100 g of butter.  
**Step 6:** In another bowl, combine the melted butter with the flour and mix.  
**Step 7:** Add very hot milk, stir well, then microwave again for 1 minute to thicken the sauce.  
**Step 8:** Flavor the sauce with a few pinches of nutmeg, a little salt, and pepper.
